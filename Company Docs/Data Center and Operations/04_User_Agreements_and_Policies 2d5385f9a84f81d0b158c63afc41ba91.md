# 5.4 User Agreements & Policies

FarmGPU operates with clear, transparent agreements designed for both individual builders and strategic partners. 

## 1. Master Service Agreement (MSA)

The foundational agreement for all FarmGPU customers. It covers:
* Terms of usage and billing.
* Intellectual property (IP) protection and data privacy.
* Liability and indemnification.
* Governing law and dispute resolution.
